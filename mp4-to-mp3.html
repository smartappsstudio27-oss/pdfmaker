How it works

Three steps, all running locally in your browser tab.

Add your MP4 files

Drag and drop up to 5 videos onto the drop zone, or click Choose Files to browse your device.

Pick a quality and convert

Choose 128, 192 or 320 kbps, then hit Convert. A small audio engine loads once and processes each file on your device.

Download your MP3s

Save each track individually, or grab all of them at once as a single zip file.

MP4 vs MP3 — what's the difference

Two very different jobs: one is a video container, the other is an audio format.

 MP4MP3
TypeVideo container (video + audio + subtitles)Audio-only format
Typical sizeLarge (tens to hundreds of MB)Small (a few MB per song)
Common useMovies, recordings, social videoMusic, podcasts, audiobooks
CompatibilityNeeds a video playerPlays almost anywhere
Editable audioLocked inside the video trackStandalone, easy to reuse

Your privacy is respected. Conversion happens entirely inside your browser using WebAssembly — your video files are never uploaded or sent to any server.
